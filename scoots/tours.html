Barge Wreck

Located opposite Vista Del Mar Hotel. From November to April, the wind blows, resulting in waves. Minimum depth of 9 meters, maximum depth of 12 meters, average visibility of 20 meters. The 30-meter long, 9-meter wide and 3-meter high ship was sunk in 1976 and is now completely enveloped in Marine life. The ship is standing on the sea floor, and there are two passageways to access it. It's a great night diving spot to see green eels and plenty of grouper.

La Ceiba Reef Preserve

Located just outside the shoreline of La Ceiba Hotel. It's a shallow dive site surrounded by a coral reef, so it's great for beginners, photographers and snorkeling. Minimum depth of 6 meters, maximum depth of 12 meters, average visibility of more than 30 meters
